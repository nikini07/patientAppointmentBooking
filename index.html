HealthLink Patient Appointment Booking

Welcome to HealthLink Clinic's online appointment booking system. Our clinic is open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. This form streamlines patient registration and appointment scheduling, addressing real-life issues like long wait times and manual paperwork with secure online submissions.

Enhanced Features: Comprehensive patient details, medical history, symptom checker, insurance info, multiple file uploads, conditional fields (e.g., for emergencies), accessibility (ARIA labels), real-time validation, privacy consent, and a warm, accessible color scheme.
